Gentle Giant
============

NO SQUASH HYOOMAN

You were sitting in your living room playing a video game and minding your own
business until you accidentally ate a RADIOACTIVE CHEETO. Now you are 50 feet
tall and you need to get out of the city while causing as little damage and
carnage as possible.
